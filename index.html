Our Story

Founded in 2020, Chai Coffee Connect began with a simple vision: to create a space where traditional chai culture meets contemporary coffee craftsmanship.

Our founders, Arjun and Meera, wanted to bring the warmth of their family's chai recipes to a modern setting, while also celebrating the artistry of specialty coffee.

Today, we're proud to be a community hub where neighbors become friends over steaming cups and meaningful conversations.
